Endovenous Laser Therapy (EVLT)

Endovenous Laser Therapy (EVLT) is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat varicose veins by using laser energy to close off and seal damaged veins. This advanced treatment offers a safe and effective alternative to traditional vein surgery. If you are suffering from varicose veins, which can cause pain, swelling, or discomfort, EVLT provides a quick and effective solution to restore vein health and improve circulation. EVLT is often recommended for patients looking for a non-surgical treatment option with faster recovery times and minimal downtime. Varicose veins, which occur when the veins in your legs become enlarged and twisted, can lead to significant discomfort, including aching, heaviness, and even skin changes.

Description

Endovenous Laser Therapy (EVLT) uses targeted laser energy to treat varicose veins by causing them to collapse and close off. This procedure is performed under local anesthesia, and the laser fiber is inserted into the vein through a small catheter. EVLT has become one of the most popular treatments for varicose veins because it is effective, minimally invasive, and has a shorter recovery period compared to traditional vein surgeries.

The procedure is relatively quick, with most patients experiencing only mild discomfort during the treatment. It is an outpatient procedure, meaning patients can typically resume normal activities soon after treatment, with significantly reduced risks and complications compared to traditional surgery.

The EVLT procedure involves the insertion of a small catheter into the affected vein. Laser energy is then emitted from the catheter, heating and closing the vein. Over time, the closed vein is naturally absorbed by the body, and blood flow is redirected to healthy veins.

The primary equipment used for EVLT is a laser fiber, which is inserted into the vein through a catheter. The catheter is connected to a laser device that generates the laser energy needed to treat the vein. Ultrasound guidance is often used to monitor the placement of the catheter and ensure accuracy during the procedure.

EVLT offers numerous benefits, including minimal discomfort, a quick recovery period, and effective results in treating varicose veins. The procedure requires no incisions and can significantly reduce symptoms such as pain, swelling, and heaviness in the legs. Additionally, patients often experience a faster return to normal activities compared to traditional vein surgery.
