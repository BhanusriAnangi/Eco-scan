Biography

Dr. Charlotte Bailey is a leading expert in MRI techniques for diagnosing complex brain and spine conditions. With her specialized knowledge in neuroimaging, she uses advanced MRI technology to detect and evaluate neurological disorders such as tumors, spinal cord injuries, and neurodegenerative diseases.

Dr. Bailey is known for her precision in diagnosing intricate conditions that require detailed imaging, which is essential for accurate treatment planning. Her focus on the brain and spine has made her a sought-after expert in both clinical and research settings.
